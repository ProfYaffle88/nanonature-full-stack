About NanoNature

Professor Yaffle

Original Gardener

Hello there! I'm so happy you're interested in NanoNature and our community. I'm an avid gardner (macro as well as nano) and I've been tending bonsai and keeping terrariums for more than 10 years. I created this site because I believe there's no greater satisfaction than watching one of your creations grow and evolve over time. NanoNature intends to provide a place for enthusiasts to create projects and post updates about their craft. Each project hosts a collection of 'entries' which will form a visual timelime for you, allowing you to reflect on your projects' progress and allowing other people to see the full scope of your vision! I want this to be a supportive community, sharing our knowledge and experience in order to help as many people as possible get started (or go further) in this fascinating hobby. All feedback welcome via any socials and especially here. This site belongs to everyone on it, so please take the time to read our Community Guidelines and How-To sections . . . and remember to help eachother grow!

How to Utilize Our Site

This key idea is is all about seeing progress over time! In this hobby, as in any long-term hobby, a great deal of satisfaction comes from seeing how something has changed. NanoNature provides a simple framework for anyone to start documenting their projects and share them with the community.

Step 1: Create your Profile -- Button in the the top right!

Step 2: Create your first Project -- Button in the top right!

You can add a name, a description and a picture (Don't panic, these can be updated later if you want)

Step 3: Create a new entry in your new project -- Button inside of the Project!

Each entry has a title, description and picture. Other community members can comment beneath these entries, so don't be afraid to ask for help!

Over time, your projects will fill up with entries creating beautiful timelines for you to track your progress and others to marvel at!

Our Community Guidelines

Welcome to the NanoNature community! This is a safe space to discuss and share all your terrarium, bonsai and indoor gardening projects. I want everyone to be welcome in our community; share knowledge and support other community members! This is a hobby area all about nurturing after all! I encourage your feedback and aim to respond to any comments as soon as possible. While I will moderate this community, I welcome open discussion. That said, each member has control over their own comment sections within their projects and I support them in moderating their own spaces; If someone has a project they've poured their heart into, they have every right to remove spam, negativity or anything else they deem unwelcome from their space. To help everyone enjoy our community, I ask that when you post, you keep in mind the following:

  • We don't allow defamatory, indecent, offensive, profane, discriminatory, misleading, unlawful or threatening comments.
  • Personal attacks, name-calling, trolling and abuse will not be tolerated.
  • Spamming or posting promotional material is not permitted.
  • Posting links to third party websites is acceptable as long as it is relevant to the discussion and not promotional or malicious.
  • We reserve the right to delete comments at our discretion and block any repeat offenders. We will remove content that is fraudulent, deceptive or misleading.
  • Coordinated group attacks and bullying will not be tolerated.
  • Respect that other people in the community have had different life experiences and may have a different perspective to yours. We welcome different viewpoints.
  • Our community is a public place. Don't post personal information that you would not be comfortable sharing with a stranger. We recommend that you don't post any information that may identify you or anyone else, such as your address, email address or phone number.

If you have a question or suggestion about NanoNature, please contact the site founder and current site admin: Professor Yaffle. There is a contact link in the About section at the top of the page and in the footer on every page.

Thank you for being excellent to eachother!
